About the Assemblywoman

Elected to represent the 14th District, Assemblywoman Official has dedicated her career to public service, advocating for transparency, economic development, and robust educational systems. Her approach to governance balances the efficiency of modern legislative practices with a deep respect for institutional traditions.

Prior to her election, she served as a managing partner in a respected civic law firm, where she specialized in public policy and community advocacy. This background instilled a rigorous, analytical approach to problem-solving that she brings to the Assembly floor every day.

She believes that effective legislation is built on clear communication and accessible services, ensuring that every constituent has a voice in the legislative process.
